Small Antlers

Where to find Small Antlers:

Small Antlers are antlers dropped by female Elk, while males drop Large Antlers instead. Elks can be found around the Skyrim wilderness, and have a 50/50 chance of dropping antlers when killed. Peddlers sometimes carry antlers as well, and a couple of samples of Small Antlers can be found in Rimerock Burrow, northwest of Dragon Bridge.
