Frost Salts

Where to find Frost Salts:

Frost Salts are a wooden bowl with white-blue contents, and are typically dropped by Frost Atronachs and Frost Thralls. Unfortunately, summoned atronachs don't drop any salts, and finding non-summoned Frost Atronachs can be tricky. Mistveil Keep in Riften, and the Grave Concoctions shop in Falkreath, will have samples of Frost Salts as well.
