Salt Pile

Where to find Salt Pile:

Salt Piles are wooden bowls with white contents. They are extremely common throughout Skyrim and can be found in buildings and dungeons alike. Alchemy vendors and innkeepers normally have several samples for sale. A large collection of Salt Piles can be found south of Merryfair Farm, northwest of Riften.
