Ash Hopper Jelly

Where to find Ash Hopper Jelly:

Added in the Dragonborn DLC, Ash Hopper Jelly is a red substance in an ornamented stone bowl. They are normally dropped by Ash Hoppers, which are found in southern Solstheim. There's one sample of the jelly downstairs in Severin Manor in Raven Rock.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Ash Hopper Jelly:

  • Blue Dartwing (Resist Shock, Restore Health)
  • Felsaad Tern Feathers (Fortify Light Armor, Restore Health)
  • Swamp Fungal Pod (Resist Shock, Restore Health)
