Taproot

Where to find Taproot:

Taproot looks like a gnarled branch with a faint green glow. It is dropped by Spriggans, which can be found outdoors in the wilderness, as well as inside caves. Samples of Taproot can be found in Proudspire Manor in Solitude, Honeyside in Riften, as well as Nightcaller Temple in Dawnstar.
