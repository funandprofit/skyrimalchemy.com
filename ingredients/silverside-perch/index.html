Silverside Perch

Where to find Silverside Perch:

Silverside Perch is a silvery fish that can be caught in lakes, ponds and rivers. They're more commonly found in shallow water: a good spot to find them is in the water around The Apprentice Stone, west-northwest of Morthal. Silverside Perch can also be found in fish barrels or sold by alchemy vendors.
