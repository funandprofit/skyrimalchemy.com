Chaurus Eggs

Where to find Chaurus Eggs:

Charus Eggs can be found in Falmer hives, and they are also dropped by Chaurus and Chaurus Reapers. They are round and glow with a pale blue light, making them easy to spot even in caves. Frostflow Lighthouse, west of Winterhold, has a huge number of Chaurus Eggs.
