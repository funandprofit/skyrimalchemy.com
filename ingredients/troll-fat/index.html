Troll Fat

Where to find Troll Fat:

Troll Fat are wooden bowls with white contents. They are dropped by trolls, which can be found everywhere in the Skyrim wilderness. Another fantastic source of Troll Fat is the East Empire Company Warehouse on the Solitude docks.

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Troll Fat:
