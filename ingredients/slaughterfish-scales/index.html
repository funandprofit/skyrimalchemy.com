Slaughterfish Scales

Where to find Slaughterfish Scales:

Slaughterfish Scales are the silvery scales of the dreaded Slaughterfish, and can be looted from their bodies. Slaughterfish can be found in most types of water, but especially in lakes. Collecitons of scale samples can be found in Tolvald's Cave, northeast of Shor's Stone, or Glenmoril Coven, west of Falkreath.
