Yellow Mountain Flower

Where to find Yellow Mountain Flower:

Added in the Dawnguard DLC, Yellow Mountain Flower look the same as blue, purple and red mountain flowers, but with yellow flowers instead. A really good spot for Yellow Mountain Flowers is in Ancestor Glade, east of Falkreath.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Yellow Mountain Flower:

  • Giant's Toe (Damage Stamina Regen, Fortify Health)
  • Wheat (Damage Stamina Regen, Fortify Health)
