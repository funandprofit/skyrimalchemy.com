Red Mountain Flower

Where to find Red Mountain Flower:

Red Mountain Flowers are, as the name suggests, red flowers. They look the same as blue and purple mountain flowers, and can be found in large droves in the foothills and below the snowline in the mountains. A lot of Red Mountain Flowers can be found in Shadowgreen Cavern, north-northwest of Solitude. They are also frequently sold by alchemy vendors.
