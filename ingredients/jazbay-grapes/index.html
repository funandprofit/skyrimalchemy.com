Jazbay Grapes

Where to find Jazbay Grapes:

Jazbay Grapes are blue-purple berries that can be harvested from the golden-colored Jazbay vines that grow in Eastmarch's rocky areas. A large number of Jazbay Grapes can be found near Steamcrag Camp and Witchmist Grove, both to the south of Windhelm.
