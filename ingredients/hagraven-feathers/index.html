Hagraven Feathers

Where to find Hagraven Feathers:

Unlike their claws, Hagraven Feathers are always dropped by Hagravens. Hagravens can often be found in the company of Forsworn, or inside of groves and lairs in the wilderness. Samples of Hagraven Feathers can be found in Hag's End, which is accessible through Deepwood Redoubt west of Solitude, as well as Witchmist Grove, south of Windhelm.
