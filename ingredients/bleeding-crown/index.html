Bleeding Crown

Where to find Bleeding Crown:

Bleeding Crown are mushrooms with red/purple caps, which are commonly found in caves. The largest source of Bleeding Crown is Chillwind Depths, a mountain cavern on the border of Hjaalmarch and The Reach.
