Histcarp

Where to find Histcarp:

Histcarp is a orange-colored fish that can be caught in lakes, ponds and rivers. A good spot to find them is in the water around The Apprentice Stone, west-northwest of Morthal. Histcarp can also be found in fish barrels or sold by alchemy vendors.
