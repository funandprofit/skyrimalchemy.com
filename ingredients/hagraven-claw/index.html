Hagraven Claw

Where to find Hagraven Claw:

Hagraven Claws are sometimes dropped by Hagravens, unlike their feathers which always drop. Hagravens can often be found in the company of Forsworn, or inside of groves and lairs in the wilderness. Samples of Hagraven Claws can be found in Grave Concoctions in Falkreath, Hall of Countenance at the College of Winterhold or The White Hall in Dawnstar.
