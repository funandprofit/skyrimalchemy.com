Ice Wraith Teeth

Where to find Ice Wraith Teeth:

Ice Wraith Teeth are icy fangs dropped by Ice Wraith. Ice Wraiths can be found in the snowy parts of Skyrim, and also live in The Midden below the College of Winterhold. There are samples of Ice Wraith Teeth in Jorrvaskr in Whiterun as well as Hall of Attainment in the College of Winterhold.
