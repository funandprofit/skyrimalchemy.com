Human Flesh

Where to find Human Flesh:

As the name describes, Human Flesh is chunks of meat off a human body and looks a lot like raw beef. Unfortunately, the ingredient cannot be looted from humans, but are found in necromancer dungeons and in locations where people have performed the black sacrement ritual to summon the Dark Brotherhood. There's a large collection of Human Flesh in Castle Volkihar (Dawnguard only); otherwise, look in Alftand, southeast of Winterhold, or Tolvald's Cave, northeast of Shor's Stone.
