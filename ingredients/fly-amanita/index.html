Fly Amanita

Where to find Fly Amanita:

Fly Amanita are mushrooms easily identified by their with red caps. They're commonly found in caves, with the largest source of Fly Amanita being Pinemoon Cave, northwest of Dragon Bridge, and Geirmund's Hall, east of Ivarstead.
