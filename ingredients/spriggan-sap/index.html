Spriggan Sap

Where to find Spriggan Sap:

Spriggan Sap is a green-blue substance in a small ceramic bowl. It can unfortunately not be harvested from Spriggans, which drop Taproots instead. Multiple samples of Spriggan Sap can be found in Proudspire Manor in Solitude, Honeyside in Riften, and the Hall of Countenance in the College of Winterhold.
