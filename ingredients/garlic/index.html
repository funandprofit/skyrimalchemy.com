Garlic

Where to find Garlic:

Garlic are normally found in houses, hanging from the ceiling in braids. The largest collections of garlic can be found in the Thalmor Embassy, north of Solitude in Haafingar, and in Boulderfall Cave, northwest of Riften. Garlic is frequently sold by vendors.
