Tundra Cotton

Where to find Tundra Cotton:

Tundra Cotton is a very common plant which has large white flowers that almost look like cotton balls (hence the name). The plant is very common in Whiterun Hold, with lots of samples available near Broken Fang Cave, west of Whiterun and Whitewatch Tower, northeast of Whiterun.

Ideal ingredient matchers:

These are ingredients which have two or more effects in common with Tundra Cotton:
