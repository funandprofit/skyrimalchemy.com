Void Salts

Where to find Void Salts:

Void Salts are a wooden bowl with dark grey contents, and are typically dropped by Storm Atronachs and Storm Thralls. Unfortunately, summoned atronachs don't drop any salts, and finding non-summoned Storm Atronachs can be tricky. The Blue Palace in Solitude, Angeline's Aromatics shop in Solitude, and Proudspire Manor in Solitude (if you've bought it) will have samples of Void Salts as well.
